

# Topcoat LLC — Ultra-Premium Luxury Flooring Website

## Design Vision
A $50,000-level luxury architecture firm website with an ultra-clean light theme, editorial layout, elegant spacing, and architectural photography style. Every image will be AI-generated using the Gemini image model for ultra-realistic, relevant flooring and surface imagery.

## Color & Typography System
- **Backgrounds**: Pure white `#ffffff` + warm light gray `#f5f5f3`
- **Accents**: Charcoal black `#111111`, warm concrete gray `#7a7a7a`, muted bronze `#8c6b4f`
- **Headings**: Playfair Display (serif, elegant)
- **Body**: Inter (clean, modern)
- **Style**: Large headings, generous letter-spacing, minimal lines, soft shadows, 6px max radius

## Animations & Interactions
- Smooth scroll, fade-in on scroll for all sections

- Hover zoom on all images
